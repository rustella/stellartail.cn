# Brand and Content

- English name: StellarTrail.

- Visual tone: morning light, clean outdoor preparation, gentle star/contour motifs.

- Outdoor skills focused on knots.
- Knot content focus: adjustable guyline hitch / 可调节帐绳结.

## Copy rules

- User-visible text belongs in `src/i18n/locales/*`.
- Keep Chinese and English copy semantically aligned.
- Do not promise unavailable capabilities.
- Do not show fake mini program QR codes. Use the coming-soon placeholder until the official QR code exists.

## Screenshot rules

- Use day-mode screenshots only.
- Keep stable asset paths under `public/assets/screenshots/`.
- Replace placeholder/generated assets with real product captures when the product runtime is available.
